# Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI

## Overview

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of Physical AI - how artificial intelligence techniques are applied to understand, interact with, and manipulate the physical world.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will:
- Understand what Physical AI is and how it differs from traditional AI
- Identify key components of Physical AI systems
- Recognize the main challenges and opportunities in Physical AI
- Appreciate the interdisciplinary nature of Physical AI
